Brenda Store

The Brenda Store opens its doors in 2020, the year of the pandemic. Started by Brenda Quizon Garcia, a businesswoman, and his family. Located in Purok 7, San Vicente, Mexico, Province of Pampanga. It wasn't a standard sari-sari market; instead, it sold frozen foods, school supplies, meats, and other items not typically found in such stores. Its objective is to provide residents of a residential neighborhood with access to a store that offers practically everything they could possibly want.
